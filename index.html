Modified Rankin Scale

mRS 0No symptoms
mRS 1No significant disability — able to perform all usual activities despite minor symptoms
mRS 2Slight disability — unable to perform all previous activities but independent in daily living
mRS 3Moderate disability — requires some help but able to walk without assistance
mRS 4Moderately severe — unable to walk or attend to own bodily needs without assistance
mRS 5Severe disability — bedridden, incontinent, requires constant nursing care

This tool is a communication scaffold for use in clinical discussions. It is not a validated prognostic calculator. Distributions are reproduced from published trial figures and reflect the characteristics of enrolled populations, which differ across trials. Values should not be applied to individual patients without consideration of eligibility criteria, patient-specific factors, and the limitations of each trial. The acceptable outcome threshold reflects a clinical judgment and is not a validated definition.
